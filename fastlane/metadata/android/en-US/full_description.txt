OConnect is a focused, open-source Android client for OpenConnect-compatible SSL VPN gateways, including Cisco AnyConnect-compatible servers and ocserv.

It keeps the proven OpenConnect connection engine while presenting profiles, connection state, and advanced options through a modern Material interface.

Features:

* OpenConnect-compatible SSL VPN connections without root
* Profile-based setup for organization, school, and self-hosted VPN gateways
* Saved credentials and automatic login for repeat connections
* RSA SecurID and TOTP software token support
* Connection log, status view, byte counters, and reconnect handling
* Quick Settings tile and stable-release update checks
* Light and dark Material UI with refreshed English and Chinese copy

OConnect requires a compatible VPN server and an account authorized to use it. It does not provide a VPN service or account by itself.
